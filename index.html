About the Event

What is HackVerse?

HackVerse 2026 is a one-day open innovation hackathon organized by GDG On Campus, bringing together students from CSE, CSE-AI, and ECE to ideate, build, and present impactful technology solutions. Participants are encouraged to leverage Google-powered tools and platforms such as Google Cloud, Firebase, Android, and web technologies to solve real-world problems through collaboration and innovation.
